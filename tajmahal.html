The Taj Mahal

A Wonder of the World

Introduction

The Taj Mahal is a beautiful white marble monument located in Agra, India. It was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

History

Construction of the Taj Mahal began in 1632 and was completed in 1653. Thousands of workers and skilled artisans were involved in building this masterpiece.

Architecture

The Taj Mahal is made of white marble and is known for its symmetry and intricate carvings. It combines elements of Islamic, Persian, and Indian architecture.

Image

Conclusion

The Taj Mahal is one of the Seven Wonders of the World and a symbol of love. It attracts millions of visitors every year.
